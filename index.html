ABOUT:

Bowlbot (keef) is a must-have discord bot for forgetful potheads like you. Use him to set intervallic reminders for you and your dumbass friends so you don't forget to toke up. He'll join your voice channel and remind you to "schmoke a bowl" at whatever rate you're feeling. You'll love his smoooth voice.

Click "toke up with me" to invite him to your server. Enjoy your sesh!

COMMANDS:

"keef help"-opens the command list
"keef [number]"-sets a schmoke interval for [number] minutes
"keef stop"-stops the interval and kicks keef from the call
"keef stats"-displays your server's schmokin' stats
"keef website"-displays website url in a fancy clickable link
"keef enable/disable rank"-enables/disables showing your server's name on website leaderboards (server admins only. rank disabled by default)
